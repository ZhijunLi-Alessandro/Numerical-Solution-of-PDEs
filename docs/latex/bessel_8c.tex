\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Implementations of complex Bessel/\+Hankel helpers and plane-\/solution utilities used by analytic test cases. 

The low-\/level functions compute J0 and Y0 via power-\/series expansions for complex arguments. Higher-\/level helpers build Hankel combinations and real plane-\/solution quantities derived from these special functions.
